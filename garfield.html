Meet Garfield

Garfield is a charming 2-year-old tabby ginger cat with a loveable personality.

He adores cuddles and spends most of his day lounging around, enjoying the comfort of a warm lap.

When it comes to food, Garfield has a favorite: lasagna! It's no surprise that he can't resist this cheesy, hearty dish.

Whether he's snuggling with his favorite human or indulging in his beloved lasagna, Garfield knows how to live life to the fullest.

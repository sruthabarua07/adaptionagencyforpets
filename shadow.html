Meet Shadow

Shadow is a 1-year-old black German Shepherd with a playful yet protective personality.

He has so much energy and enjoys running, fetching, and playing. He will enjoy tug-of-war and long walks; for sure, Shadow is always ready for an adventure.

Although playful, Shadow can also be very protective of his family members. He's the perfect combination of loving companion and guardian.

If you're in the market for a dedicated and energetic companion to include in your family, then Shadow is ready to infuse fun and security into your home.
